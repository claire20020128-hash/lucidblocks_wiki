<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival and Base Building">

Surviving the liminal expanse requires more than just building a house; it requires anchoring your soul to the world. Because the world is procedurally generated and can feel endless, setting a respawn point is your first priority.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire a Rejuvenation Anchor">
    Level up your Life and Faith stats to receive the Rejuvenation Anchor block. This is the only way to set a permanent rebirth position.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Secure a Resource Source">
    Find a Qualia instance rich in **Opal Berries** or **Tofu**. Food is scarce, and health does not regenerate automatically without specific consumables.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Construct a Shelter">
    Use **Wallpaper Blocks** or **Dirt Blocks** to create a hollowed-out space. Entities like the "Square Variant" can be brutal if you are caught in the open.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Establish a Qualia Link">
    Create a portal back to your personal Qualia plant. This ensures you have a safe haven regardless of how deep you explore into the firmament.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Food Efficiency">

<Section icon={Activity} title="Entities and Combat Tactics">

The entities in Lucid Blocks are often referred to as "esoteric critters." While some are harmless, others are relentless. In the context of **lucid blocks multiplayer** discussions, players often share strategies on how to handle the "Metal Player" and "Square Variant" enemies.

**Combat Strategy Checklist:**
- **Maintain Distance**: Most enemies have a short reach; use the "walk-back" technique.
- **Target the Head**: Entities like the Manakin take increased damage from overhead strikes.
- **Use Environmental Physics**: Many enemies can be lured into water where they sink and drown.
- **Bomb Usage**: Save Mercury Bombs for groups of Metal Players or unkillable giant creatures.

| Entity | Behavior | Notable Loot |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Square Variant** | Aggressive, fast lunges | Ball Essence, Clay |
| **Metal Player** | High HP, metallic sounds | Aluminum, Osmium |
| **Bubble Bear** | Passive unless hit | Bubble Goo |
| **The Manakin** | Stalks from a distance | Sticks, Wooden Picks |
| **The Giant** | Unkillable, destroys blocks | None (Avoid at all costs) |
