To enable **lucid blocks full screen**, simply press **F11** at any time during gameplay. This is highly recommended to fully appreciate the shader effects and the "fractal" math used in certain biomes.

| Action | Key / Input | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Full Screen** | **F11** | Toggles between windowed and full screen mode |
| **Movement** | **WASD** | Standard directional movement |
| **Jump** | **Space** | Vertical movement and climbing |
| **Crouch** | **Left Ctrl** | Prevents falling off edges (essential for the Divide) |
| **Inventory** | **E** | Opens the main menu and Apotheosis tab |
| **Break Block** | **Left Click** | Standard interaction/attack |
| **Place Block** | **Right Click** | Secondary interaction/building |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: The Safety of Crouching">
When exploring the "Divide"—the thick ceiling that separates the surface from the lower worlds—always hold **Left Ctrl**. Falling from the Divide is almost always fatal unless you have a Grappling Hook or Glider equipped.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Apotheosis Alchemy System">

Crafting in Lucid Blocks is known as **Apotheosis**. It functions more like transmutation or alchemy than traditional assembly. Matter is transferred from one state to another, often leading to unpredictable and surreal results. You don't just "build" a tool; you evoke it from the materials you've gathered.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Apotheosis Tab">
    Press **E** to open your inventory and navigate to the **Apotheosis** tab using the on-screen buttons or keyboard arrows.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Ingredients">
    Place two different ingredients into any of the slots within the circular alchemy interface. The specific slot doesn't matter, only the types and amounts of items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gain Experience">
    Every successful transmutation grants you invisible XP. When your meter fills, you will "Bloom," allowing you to upgrade your stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Retrieve the Result">
    The new item will appear in the center. Note that some recipes are interchangeable; for example, a Grappling Hook can be made with Mana Gel or Mercury Tendrils.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Result Item | Ingredient A | Ingredient B | Effect / Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Wooden Pickaxe** | Wood Block | Stick | Basic mining tool |
| **Grappling Hook** | Mana Gel | String | Essential for vertical traversal |
| **Duck Bracelet** | Sand | Wool | +5 Air, +2 HP (Equipable) |
| **Rename Pencil** | Books | String | Used to name items or entities |
| **Water Bucket** | Empty Bucket | Wood Block | Transmutes wood into liquid water |
| **Paddle** | Pickaxe | Raw Sheep Meat | A strange, "stabby" melee weapon |

<Callout type="warning" title="Inventory Transmutation on Death">
Death in Lucid Blocks is a transformative event. Everything in your inventory is randomly "apotheioized" together. You will lose your original items, but you may find yourself respawned with entirely new, rare materials crafted from your former gear.
</Callout>
