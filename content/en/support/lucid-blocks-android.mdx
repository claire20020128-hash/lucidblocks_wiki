<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Apotheosis Crafting System">

The heart of Lucid Blocks is the "Apotheosis" mechanic. Unlike standard crafting where you simply toss ingredients into a grid, Apotheosis requires a specific order of operations. This reflects the "lucid dreaming" theme, where the sequence of thoughts determines the outcome of reality.

To master Apotheosis, players must experiment with different material combinations. The order in which you interact with the crafting altar determines whether you receive a standard tool or a powerful "Apotheosized" artifact.

| Item Category | Primary Material | Secondary Material | Resulting Tool |

| **Combat** | Shadow Essence | Voxel Bone | Void Blade |
| **Utility** | Glass Lens | Pure Light | Subconscious Eye |
| **Taming** | Sweet Memory | Organic Fiber | Friendship Knot |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Crafting Tip">
Keep a journal of your crafting sequences. Some of the most powerful items in the game are hidden behind complex 5-step Apotheosis orders that are not listed in the standard recipe book.
</Callout>

The materials themselves are often found in "junk piles" throughout the world. These sprites are actually photos of real-life items, giving the game a "dollar store" aesthetic that contrasts sharply with the high-concept survival gameplay.
