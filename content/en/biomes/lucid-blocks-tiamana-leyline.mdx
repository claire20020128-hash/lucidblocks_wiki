<Section icon={Target} title="Ecosystem Manipulation & Farming">

To unlock the "Dominion" and "Apotheosis β" achievements, you must play god with the local flora and fauna. This involves a specific chain of fusions to create life forms that provide infinite resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Create Grass Blocks">
    Mix **Dirt** and **Sand** to create a Grass Block. This serves as the foundation for most organic fusions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Harvest Creature Parts">
    Kill a local creature to harvest a segment or gland. Combine this with the **Grass Block** to generate a **Bubblebear Egg**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Generate the Fruit Femme">
    Fuse the **Bubblebear Egg** with another **Grass Block** to create a **Fruit Femme Capsule**. This creature provides infinite food once grown.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Fertilization & Harvesting">
    Kill the Fruit Femme to get seeds. Plant them, use fertilizer to unlock **Dominion α**, and wait for it to grow a pearl to unlock **Dominion β**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Infinite Food Loop">
