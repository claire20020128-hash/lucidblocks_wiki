<Section icon={Target} title="How to Craft Your First Cache Cube">

The Cache Cube is the most reliable **lucid blocks chest** for mid-game progression. It provides a clean UI and sufficient slots for bulk resource management. Follow these steps to secure your first high-tier storage unit.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Magnetite">
    Locate Magnetite in rocky biomes or underground caves. You will need at least one piece for every Cache Cube or Funnel you intend to build.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Prismatic Cubes">
    Prismatic Cubes are often found in higher elevation biomes or through the fusion of Mana Gem terrain blocks. These act as the "stabilizer" for the Cache Cube.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Access the Apotheosis UI">
    Open your crafting interface and place 1 Magnetite and 1 Prismatic Cube into the slots. Do not add any other "essence" items to avoid mutation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Finalize the Fusion">
    Initiate the crafting process. You will receive 1 Cache Cube storage block, which can be placed anywhere in your base to start storing items.
  </Step>
</Steps>
