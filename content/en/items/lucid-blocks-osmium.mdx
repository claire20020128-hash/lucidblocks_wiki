The fusion UI looks at the collective properties of the items you sacrifice. If you mix Osmium with organic materials like sticks or wood, the system stabilizes into functional tools. However, adding too many disparate elements can lead to "dilution," where the core property of the Osmium is lost, resulting in useless junk or bizarre artifacts with unpredictable effects.

When using the Apotheosis system, you do not always need to fill all six slots. Using fewer items often leads to more stable, basic tools like the **Frost Pick**, whereas filling all slots increases the chance of a "mutation" that might ruin your rare materials.

<Section icon={Map} title="The Frost Pick: Your First Major Milestone">

The most frequent question regarding **lucid blocks osmium** is how to effectively turn it into a mining tool. The **Frost Pick** is widely considered the best early-game unlock because it harvests common blocks significantly faster than the basic Wooden or Leaf picks. This acceleration allows you to gather the necessary materials for base building and advanced fusion much earlier than players who stick to traditional paths.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Raw Materials">
    Locate Osmium deposits in the starting biomes. You will also need a basic supply of **Sticks**, which can be harvested from trees or by using a **Leaf Pick** on foliage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access the Apotheosis UI">
    Open your crafting interface. Place one **Osmium** block and one **Stick** into the fusion slots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Fusion">
    Initiate the Apotheosis process. The result should be a **Frost Pick** (sometimes referred to as an Ice Pick). Record this in your journal immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Accelerate Mining">
    Equip the Frost Pick to begin mining Aluminum and Magnetite. These resources are required for the next tier of weapons and mobility gear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Tool Name | Ingredients | Best Use Case |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Wooden Pick** | Stick + Dirt/Log | Very early gathering |
| **Leaf Pick** | Stick + Wooden Pick | Harvesting sticks/berries |
| **Frost Pick** | Stick + Osmium | Fast general mining |
| **Bone Pick** | Bone + String | High durability mining |
| **Pickaxe** | Wooden Pick + Aluminum (x3) | Heavy duty excavation |

<Callout type="warning" title="Durability Warning">
While the **Frost Pick** is exceptionally fast, its durability is lower than the Aluminum Pickaxe. Always keep a spare Osmium block in your inventory to craft a replacement if you are far from your base.
</Callout>
