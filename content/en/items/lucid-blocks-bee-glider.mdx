### Finding Feathers
Feathers are the "bottleneck" resource for the Bee Glove. They are primarily found within cardboard boxes scattered throughout the map. These boxes are destructible objects that often hide various crafting components.
- **Location**: Found in abandoned structures, near campsites, and inside buildings.
- **Method**: Use any tool or weapon to break the box; the loot will drop automatically.
- **Pro Tip**: Don't just walk past boxes—every box is a potential Feather source.

### Collecting Wool
Wool is significantly easier to obtain and is often collected during standard exploration.
- **Location**: Found in general loot containers, chests, and as a byproduct of gathering resources in grassy biomes.
- **Inventory Management**: Keep at least one Wool in your stash at all times so you are ready the moment a Feather drops.

| Location Type | Likely Loot | Interaction |

| **Abandoned Camps** | Wool, Scraps | Search Containers |
