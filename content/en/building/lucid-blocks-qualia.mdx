  <Step num="2" title="Create Feathers">

  <Step num="3" title="Assemble the Glider">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade to Bee Glider">
    If you find a Bee block or specific insectoid glands, fuse them with your Glider Glove to create the Bee Glider for improved durability and height.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Target} title="Biomes, Creatures, and Environmental Hazards">

The world is divided into surreal biomes, each with its own set of rules. While the "Fog Meadow" is relatively safe, other areas like the "Essence Morass" are made entirely of meat pulp and can be quite unsettling. Understanding the hazards of each region is key to maintaining your HP and equipment durability.

| Biome Name | Key Resources | Major Threats |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Fog Meadow** | Wood, Grass, Sheep | Manikins, Low visibility |
| **Essence Morass** | Meat Pulp, Glands | Giant Spiders, Slimes |
| **Nuclear Core** | Graphite Rods, Magnetite | Radiation sickness, Seizures |
| **Plastic Land** | Plastic Blocks | High-Dimensional Beings |
| **Pleasant Forest** | Rare Seeds, Flowers | Bubblebears (Neutral) |

<Callout type="warning" title="Radiation Danger">
If you see glowing green rods or graphite rods, do not touch them without protective gear. Radiation sickness will turn your UI green and rapidly drain your Max HP. If you are infected, return to your **lucid blocks qualia** immediately to recover.
</Callout>

Creatures in Lucid Blocks range from the innocent Bubblebear to the terrifying Blasphemous Creature. Some enemies, like the Manikins, can be farmed for wands, while others, like the giant sky cubes, are better left alone until you have endgame gear.

</Section>
