### Understanding Essence Logic
Every item in the game possesses a hidden essence value. When you combine items, the game calculates the dominant essence to determine the output. If you mix conflicting essences, you often end up with "junk" or bizarre artifacts that may have unintended side effects.

| Essence Type | Common Sources | Resulting Category |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Organic** | Sticks, Leaves, Wool | Basic Tools, Utility Gear |
| **Mineral** | Pebble Blocks, Magnetite | Heavy Weapons, Mining Tools |
| **Ethereal** | Glands, Mercury Tendrils | Magic Wands, Artifacts |
| **Volatile** | Sulfur, Glaggle Tokens | Explosives, Fire Weapons |

### Tool Progression Steps
To move from basic survival to efficient resource gathering, follow this specific progression path:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Stick Phase">
    Gather sticks from the ground or by breaking small bushes. Combine a **Stick** with any common block (Dirt/Sand) to create your first **Wooden Pick**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Bone Upgrade">
    Hunt small mobs or find remains to acquire **Bones**. Combine a **Bone** with **String** (from sheep or world drops) to create the **Bone Pick**, which is significantly more durable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Metal Integration">
    Once you find **Aluminum Blocks**, fuse them with your existing Wooden Pick to upgrade it into a standard **Pickaxe**. This allows you to mine harder materials like **Magnetite**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
